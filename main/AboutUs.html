Company Achievements

At OHMS, we are committed to delivering exceptional healthcare through our expertise, innovation, and patient-centered approach. Since our founding, we have treated over 1,000 patients, contributing to healthier lives in our community. Our achievements include pioneering advanced diagnostic methods, introducing cutting-edge treatments, and achieving a 95% patient satisfaction rate. Our dedicated specialists continually learn and present research at national and international medical forums, ensuring we stay at the forefront of healthcare innovation. With these accomplishments, we remain focused on providing compassionate, high-quality care that makes a difference.

History

Founded in 2024, OHMS began as a small family practice committed to personalized, high-quality healthcare. Over the years, we evolved into a comprehensive medical center, expanding our services to include specialties like pediatrics, dermatology, and cardiology. Our move to a larger facility in 2024, equipped with state-of-the-art technology, marked a significant milestone in our journey. Throughout our growth, we have remained dedicated to compassionate, patient-centered care and are proud to be a trusted healthcare provider in the region, committed to improving the health and wellness of our community.
